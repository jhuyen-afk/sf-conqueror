⚡️ JHSF Conqueror

Quick Start:

  1. Open Goose
  2. Copy and paste this command:
    3. JHSF Conqueror

First Time Setup:

  1. Enable Mac Permissions:
    • Open System Preferences
    • Go to Security & Privacy > Privacy
    • Enable Accessibility permissions
    • Allow Chrome automation
  2. Get Your Salesforce URL:
    • Log into Salesforce
    • Go to Leads tab
    • Click "New"
    • Select "US Sales"
    • Click "Next"
    • Copy the entire URL from your browser

CSV Format:

Company Name,Address,City,State,Zip,Phone Grand Avenue Pizza,34-24 30th Avenue,Queens,NY,11103,(718) 728-8879
Important Notes:
  • All leads are created under the "Beauty" category
  • No GPV revenue is added - you must audit and add this after creation
  • Phone numbers must include area code and parentheses: (XXX) XXX-XXXX
  • State must be two-letter code: NY, CA, etc.
  • Have your CSV file ready to share with Goose when prompted

Process:

  1. Type "JHSF Conqueror" to start
  2. Tell Goose if you're a new user
  3. For new users:
    • Provide your Salesforce URL
    • Choose your own custom command (optional)
  4. Share your CSV file when prompted
  5. Keep your Salesforce window visible
  6. Let the automation run
  7. Audit leads afterward to add GPV revenue
