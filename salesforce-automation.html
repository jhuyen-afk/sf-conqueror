⚡ Standard Workflow

  1. Navigate to direct URL (5 seconds)
  2. Zoom out (Cmd+Minus 20x) (5 seconds)
  3. Tab to First Name (~30 tabs)
  4. Fill 10 required fields sequentially (2-3 minutes)
  5. Navigate to Save button (Shift+Tab 4x from bottom)
  6. Save (5 seconds)
  7. Close tab (Cmd+W)
