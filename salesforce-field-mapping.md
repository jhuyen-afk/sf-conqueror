## FIELD RECOGNITION TIPS

### How to Identify Where You Are:
1. **Look for section headers** - they're bold and larger text
2. **Check field labels** - they're above or to the left of input fields
3. **Required fields** have asterisks (*) and show red borders when empty
4. **Dropdowns** show "--None--" or a default value with a dropdown arrow
5. **Text areas** are taller than regular text fields
6. **Checkboxes** are small squares
7. **Lookup fields** have search icons and "Search..." placeholders
8. **Date fields** have calendar icons
9. **Info icons** (ℹ️) appear next to some field labels

### Visual Cues:
- **Blue border** = currently focused field
- **Red border** = required field that's empty
- **Gray text** = placeholder text in empty fields
- **Bold text** = section headers

## AUTOMATION STRATEGY

### For Filling Out Forms:
1. **Start from Merchant Token** (first field)
2. **Tab through fields** in order
3. **Recognize each field by its label** (not by tab count)
4. **Type values** when you reach the correct field
5. **For dropdowns**: Type the value and press Enter
6. **For checkboxes**: Press Space to toggle
7. **Skip optional fields** by just pressing Tab
8. **Verify required fields** are filled before saving

### Key Commands:
- `Tab` - Move to next field
- `Shift+Tab` - Move to previous field
- `Enter` - Submit dropdown selection or click focused button
- `Space` - Toggle checkbox
- `Escape` - Close dropdown without selection
